Project Description

I joined Bluemix in May of 2015 to a new team focused on Disvoer, Try and Buy. We wanted to figured ouut how to best get users in the door of the product and make their lives easier in the long run. But one of the biggest annoyances for most of our users what the fact that we lacked adequate means to give them support. User's where not sure on how to contact sales, how to go about submitting a support ticket, nor where they able to understand the external support options.

So that was our fist task on our newly formed team, how to make a more delightful, user friendly way to submit a support ticket, find information on our docs site and the Stack Overflow site, as well as contact support.

Below is where we started, some initial research and wireframes done months prior to me joining the team, sketches on synthesising new ideas, and the final results.

Finding a better solution for support

A lot of the initial research, wireframing and thinking was done by Daniel early on, I only joined after he'd been put onto another project. Using his wireframes and research as a catalyst for idendifying the best way to build out a support tool, I started with sketching and initial ideation based on his initial intent.

The goal for this pane was to open initially from the bottom right, and just be a support tool. After talkign to a few developers around the studio I wanted to make this sytem more robust and something that could grow with the product.

Growing upon the initial concept

I took what Daniel had proposed, and grew it to encompass more aspects then just filing a support ticket. We added a notification section, the ability to quickly toggle between the user's orgs and spaces, search docs and Stack Overflow and submit a support ticket or contact sales directly within a small side pannel.
